One year of COVID-19 in Indiana

It's been nearly a year since Indiana reported its first case and first death from COVID-19, and we’re still in the middle of our "new normal." Indiana Public Broadcasting and Side Effects Public Media want to hear your stories about what’s changed for you in the last year. We're gathering stories of the people we've lost, the triumphs, the recoveries, and the changes Hoosiers have experienced in the last year.
