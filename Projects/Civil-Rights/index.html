Latino students account for 10 percent of Indiana's student population.

Hispanic students account for 10 percent of suspensions, or one out of 14 Hispanic students are suspended

Hispanic students account for 9 percent of arrests, or one out of 11 arrests are a Hispanic student

Black students account for 12 percent of Indiana's student population.
